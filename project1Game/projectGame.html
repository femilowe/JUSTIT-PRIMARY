How To Play

Click on the game screen to choose the game
then press the spacebar to start.

You play as the StarMan.

Using the keyboard... press the LEFT, RIGHT and UP arrow keys to move.

Collect STARS and DIAMONDS for points.

Last until the TIMER runs out for more points.

Watch out for the ROBOT, if it gets you its GAMEOVER!
